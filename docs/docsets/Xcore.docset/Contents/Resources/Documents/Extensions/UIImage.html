  • colorize(_:)

    Colorize image with given tint color.

    This is similar to Photoshop’s Color layer blend mode. This is perfect for non-greyscale source images, and images that have both highlights and shadows that should be preserved. White will stay white and black will stay black as the lightness of the image is preserved.
